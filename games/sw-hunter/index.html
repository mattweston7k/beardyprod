Instructions

The objective of the game is to find the hidden base.

The first player to discover it wins the game.

Player 1 will choose their side rebel/empire and then player 2 will be assigned the other

You can move the targeted search area by dragging the rectangle on the map.
The playarea where the target is hidden, will highlighted with a transparent holding box.

After each search, you will be given feedback if you were in the surroundings of the target.

The Empire has a more precise feedback, but will need to be closer for it to calculate.

The Rebels can search 2x / turn, evening out the disadvantage of feedback.
However the search area for their reticule is much smaller.

After your turn, the searches that have been marked as in vicinity of the base, are highlighted with different color for each faction

For details on Empire markers, click the "?" button inside the game

Choose your faction, player 1

Click on card to see the perks for that faction
